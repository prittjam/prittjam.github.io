About

I am a Post-Doctoral Research Scientist at Facebook Reality Labs working with Michal Perdoch, where I am responsible for conducting research in the geometric calibration and auto-calibration of head-mounted capture systems. I am also an adviser for The Machine Learning Lab at Ukrainian Catholic University in Lviv. Prior I worked in The Applied Algebra and Geometry Group at The Czech Institute for Informatics, Robotics, and Cybernetics (CIIRC) led by Tomas Pajdla.

My research is on robust multi-model estimation and minimal solvers with applications to geometric camera calibration, scene-plane rectification and modeling repeated scene content. I received my B.Sc. in Mathematics at The University of North Texas and my M.Sc. in Computer Science from Czech Technical University in Prague with distinction.
