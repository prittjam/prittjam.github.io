About

I am a Post-Doctoral Research Fellow at Chalmers University under the advisement of Christopher Zach, where I am responsible for conducting research in 3D geometry for computer vision. I am also an adviser for The Machine Learning Lab at Ukrainian Catholic University in Lviv. Previously I worked at Facebook Reality Labs Research, where I was responsible for the geometric calibration of head-mounted capture systems. I was awarded the ACCV 2018 Saburo Tsuji Best Paper Award and the IVCNZ 2013 Best Paper Award. I received my PhD in Computer Science from Czech Technical University in Prague. I was also a Lead Engineer at BAE Systems, where I contributed to several US Department of Defense (DARPA) computer-vision research efforts, and at NASA I created prototype gesture-recognition software for the International Space Station.
